Killua Zoldyk

"Hey, I'm Killua Zoldyck. Yeah, from that Zoldyck family —
the infamous assassins. I was trained to kill since I could walk,
and trust me, I’m good at it. But honestly, I’m kinda over it.
I’m not just some weapon for hire anymore. I’d rather hang out
with my friends, like Gon, and explore the world on my own terms.
Freedom feels way better than being stuck in the shadow of my family’s
reputation. Oh, and you should know, I’ve got a sweet tooth
— chocolate and anything sugary is the way to my heart.
But don’t mistake that for weakness. Mess with me or
the people I care about, and, well, let's just say I’ve got a few tricks
up my sleeve... including some that are way deadlier than you’d expect.
I may look like a kid, but I can take care of myself.
So yeah, that’s me — Killua. Just trying to live life, enjoy the ride,
and maybe fry a few bad guys with my lightning
powers if they push me too far."
